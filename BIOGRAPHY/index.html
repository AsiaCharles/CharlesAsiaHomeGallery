Childhood Years

Childhood Memories

I was born in San Juan City and grew up in a small but happy environment.1 When I was a kid, sobrang hilig ko sa basketball dati, kaya lagi akong excited pag may time para gawin iyon.2 My family was always supportive, kahit minsan makulit ako at mababa grades ko.3 I remember spending most of my days playing with my friends sa kalsada or sa bakuran.4 Madalas kaming maglaro ng basketball sa bakuran namin since may rim naman nakasabit doon at doon kami naglalaro, at doon nagsimula yung mga physical activities na ginagawa ko.5 My childhood was filled with moments na sobrang simple pero sobrang saya.6

I also learned basic responsibilities dahil tinuturuan ako ng parents ko.7 Minsan stubborn ako, pero natuto rin ako unti-unti.8 School days were fun kahit may mga times na tinatamad ako gumising.9 I had teachers who inspired me in different ways, kahit pinapaiyak ako.10 I enjoyed learning new things kahit minsan nakakabaliw yung assignments.11 Mahilig din akong makipagkulitan sa mga classmates ko.12

Every birthday celebration ko noon simple lang pero memorable.13 I grew up playing mostly on the computer like FPS games or other games trending that time.14 Tinuturuan ako ng mga kuya sa FPS games kaya may experience ako sa teenage life ko.15 Nakakatakot maging bata minsan pero exciting din.16 Marami akong natutunan sa mga maliit na experiences ko.17 Those childhood moments helped shape who I am today.18 Kahit hindi perfect ang childhood ko, sobrang thankful ako sa lahat ng memories.19 Overall, my childhood was a mix of fun, lessons, and discoveries that I'll never forget.20

Teenage Years

My teenage years were a roller coaster, as in minsan chill, minsan chaotic.1 Entering high school felt like starting a new chapter.2 I met new friends who made those years unforgettable.3 Dito ko rin na-experience yung unang stress sa academics.4 Pero syempre, kasama rin yung mga kalokohan with friends.5 I became more curious about the world and about myself.6 Nag-start na rin yung pressure sa studies and expectations.7 Sometimes I felt lost, pero normal naman daw yun sabi nila.8

I also discovered new hobbies like gala sa iba't ibang places during my teenage years.9 This was also the time that I started exploring my dreams seriously.10 Nagkaroon ako ng mga insecurities tulad ng ibang teenagers.11 Pero natuto rin akong i-accept yung sarili ko little by little.12 Then the pandemic happened, and everything suddenly changed.13 Bigla kaming nag-shift to online classes, at sobrang hirap mag-adjust sa setup.14 Nakaka-miss ang friends dahil hindi kami makalabas at magkita.15

Pero dahil sa pandemic, natuto akong maging mas patient at mas independent sa pag-aaral.16 The situation was scary, pero tinuruan ako nito na maging grateful sa small things.17 It also made me realize how important family and health really are.18 Looking back, ang daming lessons that prepared me for the future.19 Those teenage experiences, including the pandemic, helped me understand who I want to become.20

College Years

College Journey

My college journey started with a mix of excitement and kaba.1 On my first day, hindi ko alam kung saan ako pupunta, pero nag-pretend akong confident.2 College life felt different kasi mas independent na ako.3 I met new classmates who eventually became close friends.4 Dito ko naramdaman yung tunay na "adulting" moments.5 The workload got heavier, but I learned how to handle it.6 Marami ring group projects na minsan nakaka-stress pero nakaka-enjoy.7 I discovered more about my strengths and weaknesses.8

My professors challenged me to improve and grow.9 Sometimes I felt overwhelmed, pero tinutulungan ako ng friends ko.10 I also joined activities and organizations that helped me build confidence.11 College taught me how to balance fun and responsibilities.12 Nakakapagod pero fulfilling lalo na pag may na-achieve ako.13 I gained experiences that I know will help me sa future job ko.14 There were days na gusto ko nang sumuko, pero laban lang talaga.15

Every semester felt like a new adventure na may ups and downs.16 I learned how to manage my time better through deadlines and schedules.17 College life made me stronger and more independent.18 It helped me discover my path at kung ano talaga gusto ko sa buhay.19 Overall, college shaped me into a more mature and determined person.20
